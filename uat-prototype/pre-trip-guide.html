PRE-TRAVEL

Do this before you start the trip

Use this page before travel to confirm the exact final destination. If you have a PC, printer, or extra time, prepare map backups too.

The most important action is choosing the correct Final Destination in Google Maps. Printouts and screenshots are optional backups. During travel, use live Google Maps again because routes, times, and transport options can change.

If you cannot use Google Maps in real time, prepare screenshots that you can show to another person. Use different zoom levels so they can understand both the whole trip and the exact local place.

Before Travel

1. Open the exact place in Google Maps

Check the pin, address, area, and place type. Same-name places can exist in different cities.

Optional Backup

2. Save the route option screen

Capture the route option, transport mode, and rough travel time. This is a reference, not a fixed instruction.

Optional Backup

3. Save a zoomed-out route overview

This helps another person understand the whole trip shape if you cannot use Google Maps in real time. It may not show exact platforms, bus stop sides, or entrances.

Optional Backup

4. Save a detailed local view

This helps another person understand the exact station, bus stop, or entrance area. It may not show the whole trip, so keep the overview too.

Prepare these before leaving

  • Exact final destination name
  • Area, city, or prefecture
  • Place type, such as temple, hotel, station, park, or museum
  • Optional: Google Maps place page printout or screenshot
  • Optional: route option, overview, and detailed local screenshots

When asking someone before travel

私の目的地はこの場所で合っていますか。Google Maps上で正しく表示されていますか。

Is this my correct destination? Is it shown correctly on Google Maps?
